Common Computer Problems and How to Fix Them

Slow computers, startup errors, and unexpected crashes are common issues for both home and business users. This guide covers practical troubleshooting tips for everyday problems.

Typical Computer Issues

  • Computer running slowly: clear cache, uninstall unused programs, check startup items
  • Frequent crashes: check for hardware or driver issues
  • Software errors: reinstall or update programs
  • Network connectivity issues: restart router, check settings, update drivers

Following these steps can save time and avoid unnecessary IT calls.
