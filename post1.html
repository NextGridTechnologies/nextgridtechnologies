5 Microsoft 365 Security Mistakes Small Businesses Make

Many businesses assume Microsoft 365 is secure by default. Avoid these common mistakes to protect your environment.

Common Microsoft 365 Security Mistakes

  • Not Enforcing Multi-Factor Authentication – Without MFA, accounts are highly vulnerable to credential theft.
  • Too Many Global Administrators – Excess admin privileges increase risk exposure.
  • Default Spam Policies – Default protection is often not enough against modern phishing attacks.
  • No Conditional Access Policies – Users can log in from anywhere without restrictions.
  • Oversharing in OneDrive & SharePoint – External sharing can expose sensitive company data.
