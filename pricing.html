Pricing FAQ

What counts as a "user"?

Any staff member with a PC/laptop + email account. Shared PCs count as 1 user. We don’t charge for printers or phones.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. 30 days notice. No penalties. We’ll export all your passwords, docs, and configs on exit.

What if I have 7 users?

You pay 7 × R1,800 = R12,600/mo on Business plan. Minimum is 5 users for managed plans. Fewer than 5? Use R950 check + break-fix.

Do you support Mac?

Yes. +R200/user/mo for Mac management via Jamf/Mosyle. We’ll tell you upfront if we can’t support it.
