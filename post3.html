Why Every Small Business Needs a Zero‑Trust Approach

Zero-trust security means “never trust, always verify.” This approach assumes no user or device is trusted by default, even inside the network. Learn why small businesses should adopt zero-trust policies and how it strengthens cybersecurity.

Benefits of Zero-Trust Security

  • Minimizes insider threats
  • Limits lateral movement in networks
  • Protects sensitive data
  • Enhances cloud and remote work security

Zero-trust can be implemented through multi-factor authentication, strict access controls, and continuous monitoring.
